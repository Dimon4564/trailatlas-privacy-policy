Data Collection

Trailatlas is an Android mobile application designed to encourage bicycle riding by showing trails and related content. The app supports Google Sign-In (Firebase) and a rewarded coin system.

  • Account information: We collect the email address obtained via Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication).
  • Location / GPS: The app uses the device GPS to display tracks and provide navigation while the app is in use. Precise location data are processed on-device for map display and navigation and are not saved on our servers or transferred to third parties.
  • Usage and technical data: Basic technical data required for app operation and diagnostics (e.g., device model, OS version, app version, crash reports) may be processed by Firebase services.
  • Sensitive data: We do not collect sensitive categories of personal data (health, religion, biometrics, etc.).

How We Use Data

  • Authentication and account management (via Google/Firebase).
  • Support and communication (responding to user inquiries at the contact email).
  • Providing core app features: displaying GPS location while the app runs, navigation, and track display; enabling user-created content and in-app rewards.
  • Analytics and diagnostics (Firebase) to improve app stability and performance.
  • Advertising: showing rewarded ads (AdMob) when the user opts to view an ad to receive coins.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, data necessary for app operation may be processed by Google services (Firebase, AdMob). Third-party SDKs (such as Firebase and AdMob) may collect device identifiers and other technical data under their own privacy policies. Ads may use data for personalization (AdMob).
